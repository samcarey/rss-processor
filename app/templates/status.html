About

This RSS Podcast Processor automatically downloads podcast episodes, detects duplicate audio segments (like ads, intros, and outros), and removes them to create cleaned episodes. The cleaned episodes are served via custom RSS feeds that you can add to your favorite podcast app.

The background worker checks for new episodes every 30 minutes and processes them automatically. New podcasts are backfilled with episodes from the last 2 weeks.
